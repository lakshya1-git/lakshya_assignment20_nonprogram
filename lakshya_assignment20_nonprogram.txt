5. Read books.csv and insert its data into the book table.
Answer->
import csv
import sqlite3
ins_str = 'insert into book values(?, ?, ?)'
with open('books.csv', 'rt') as infile:
    books = csv.DictReader(infile)
    for book in books:
        curs.execute(ins_str, (book['title'], book['author'], book['year']))
db.commit()
